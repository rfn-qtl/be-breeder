Be-Breeder 2.0 is a R/Shiny application for statistical analysis and plant breeding developed 
by Dr. Roberto Fritsche Neto and Ph.D. students Filipe Inacio Matias and Julia Silva Morosini, 
who integrate the Laboratory of Allogamous Plant Breeding group - Department of Genetics, 
"Luiz de Queiroz" College of Agriculture, University of Sao Paulo, Brazil.


This app is free for online use, just the intellectual property is reserved in periodicals by 
scientific citations of Be-Breeder papers.
